College Life

I am a current student at Mount Saint Mary's University studying Mathematics and Computer Science. I have always performed well at Math and have developed a love for it in College. While at Mount St. Mary's I have done many different activities. During my first two years of college I was on the D1 swim team. I was continuing my swimming career but I got a spinal injury that finally made me hang up my cap and goggles. I love swimming with all my heart and would deffinetly do those 14 years of my life again.

Ending my swimming career might have been one of the best things that could have happened to me. It allowed me to have a ton more free time and that allowed me to get involved with so many different activies. One of the first things I ended up doing was getting several differnt jobs on campus. Some of them being a head peer tutor, a student instructor for various math courses, and being an Admissions Student Recuiter. One of the other big activies that I got involed with is Makers House. Makers House is a place where you can be creative and build things. It has everything you could imagine. It has a Woodshop, Craft lab, Ceramics, Fab lab, and more. It is a place that I met some of my best friedns there, and quickly became my favorite place on Campus.

After I graduate I am joining an organization called Fellowship Of Catholic University Students (FOCUS). I plan to be in FOCUS for two years and then look into getting a job dealing data anyalitcs.
